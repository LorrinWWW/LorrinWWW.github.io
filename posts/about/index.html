Jue Wang

Hello, I am currently a postdoc/research scientist at Together AI, under the guidance of Prof. Ce Zhang. Before that, I got my Ph.D. degree from Zhejiang University, advised by Prof. Lidan Shou.

My recent research mainly focuses on efficient and cost-effective algorithms and systems for LLMs:

  • Effecient Inference for Language Models
    • Self-Spec (ACL24), Compress & Prompt (ICML24), Deja Vu (ICML23, Oral), SkipBERT (ACL22)
  • Efficient Distributed Training Systems at Scale
    • CocktailSGD (ICML23), AQ-SGD (NeurIPS22)
  • Cost-Effective Algorithms for Enhancing LLMs
    • Mixture-of-Agents (ICLR25), Skill-it! (NeurIPS23, Spotlight)
